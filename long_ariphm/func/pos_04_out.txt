This program multiply real and integer
Real consists of mantissa and exponent. Number of digits in mantissa can not be more than 30.
Exponent is integer between -99999 and 99999. Real is <mantissa>*10^<exponent>.
Number of digits in integer can not be more than 30. Mantissa examples: 1.234; .01; -.2; 123.; 123
Exponent examples: 0; 10; -12. Integer examples: 0; 10; -12
